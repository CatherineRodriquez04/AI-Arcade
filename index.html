Tic Tac Toe Game Summary:

This is a JavaScript implementation of the classic TicTacToe game. The game allows players to play against each other, play against an AI, or watch two AI players compete against each other. The AI uses the minimax algorithm with alpha-beta pruning to determine the best moves. Choose the game mode and follow the prompts to play. ENJOY!ðŸ˜Š

Directions:

1. Choose player1/player2 symbol ('X' or 'O')

2. Choose your game mode (Two Player, Against Ai, Ai vs. Ai)

3. Select the box to place your mark on the board

4. Wait for player2 to place their mark on the board

5. Press 'reset' to restart the game
